Welcome to Pokéhoops!

Pokéhoops is a captivating web application that combines the world of NBA basketball with the excitement of Pokémon. By leveraging NBA player stats, it generates a personalized statistic score for every active NBA player based on my custom P.O.K.E.D. statistic (Power, Offense, Kinetics, Endurance, Defense).

You can favorite players, build a hypothetical starting 5, calculate that team's POKED and view current team rosters/player info from 22-23 NBA season.

So what are you waiting for? Let's see the players!
